Decision Archive is a demo project for documenting decisions instead of only collecting notes.

It shows how project pages can explain context, status, structure, and the reason a project exists.

## Format

Each decision entry contains:

- Context
- Options considered
- Decision
- Reasoning
- Expected outcome
- Follow-up notes

## Why it belongs in the demo

It demonstrates that SignalPaper projects can be more than portfolio cards. They can be living records of how work develops.
