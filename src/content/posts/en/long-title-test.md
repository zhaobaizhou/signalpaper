This post exists to test title wrapping behavior in the post list and post detail views.

The title is intentionally long to verify that:

1. The post list renders long titles without layout overflow
2. The post detail hero wraps gracefully at all viewport sizes
3. No truncation occurs unless explicitly intended

If the layout handles this title well, it will handle most real article titles.
